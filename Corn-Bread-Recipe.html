Corn Bread Instructions

Step 1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Step 2. Pour 1 package of Jiffy corn muffin mix into a mixing bowl.
Step 3. Add one egg, 1/4 cup milk, half a can of sweet creamed corn, and a dollop of sour cream.
Step 4. Mix together until creamy and pour into a 9 inch cast iron skillet (or whatever pan you have).
Step 5. Place in oven for 15 minutes and test for done-ness by inserting a butternife or toothpick into the center of the pan. If there is batter on the knife or toothpick when you remove it, then it needs another ~5 minutes.
Step 6. Once the knife or toothpick comes out clean / dry, remove the pan from the oven to cool. Step 7. Lather the top of the corn bread with butter and voila!
